Who I am

I started out as an investment banker, working and advising large companies on mergers & acquisitions.

After 3 years, I turned my back on the industry and started working on ideas that I was really passionate about. I founded two start-ups - of which one failed badly and the other one less badly - and spent my time focusing on getting the right V.1 product out of the door to make my users happy.

Ever since this great experience, I have been working with other entrepreneurs to realize their product vision - and I could not imagine a more fulfilling job.

When I am not trying to conceive the next big product idea, I love spending my time outdoors, traveling or playing football.
